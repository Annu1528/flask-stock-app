ABES ENGINEERING COLLEGE, GHAZIABAD

About ABES Engineering College , Ghaziabad

ABES Engineering College is a private engineering and management institute located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India, known for its strong placement record and commitment to quality education. Established in 2000, it is ranked as the top engineering college in AKTU (APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University) in the Delhi NCR region. The college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E./B.Tech., M.E./M.Tech., MCA, and MBA, with a focus on engineering disciplines like Computer Science, Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil, as well as specialized areas like Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence.
Key Features and Highlights:
Strong Placement Record:
ABES Engineering College is known for its high placement rates, particularly in engineering fields like Computer Science and Information Technology, with some students securing placement offers of up to 59.91 LPA. Diverse Programs:
The college offers a range of courses beyond engineering, including M.Tech, MCA, and MBA programs. Emphasis on Quality Education:
ABES emphasizes academic excellence and strives to provide a transformative education that prepares students for leadership roles. Extensive Infrastructure:
The campus boasts modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, a library, and various amenities like hostels, medical support, and a sports complex. Focus on Innovation and Research: The college encourages research and innovation, with a dedicated Center of Excellence for Teaching and Learning (CETL) and a focus on developing cutting-edge technologies. Strong Alumni Network:
ABES has a large and active alumni network, with over 18,000 alumni worldwide. Commitment to Social Responsibility:
The college is involved in various social responsibility initiatives, including promoting environmental awareness and supporting local communities.
Educational Philosophy:
ABES Engineering College believes in nurturing not only academic excellence but also a seamless transition into the professional world. The college emphasizes the importance of human values, academic excellence, and composite personality development programs for enhancing the potential of individual students. The focus is on developing students' skills and knowledge to make them effective contributors to society. ABES also emphasizes the development of professional ethics and a positive attitude in its students.
