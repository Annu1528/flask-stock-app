ABES ENGINEERING COLLEGE, GHAZIABAD

ABES is a private engineering and business school located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. It offers a variety of programs, including B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and MCA, and is known for its strong focus on practical education and industry partnerships. The institution is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and is accredited by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Key Aspects of ABES: Programs: ABES offers a wide range of programs, including B.Tech in various engineering disciplines (Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical), M.Tech, MBA, and MCA. Accreditations: The college is approved by AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Government of India and is ISO 9001:2015 certified. NIRF Ranking: ABES has been recognized by the National Institute Ranking Framework (NIRF).
